Below are the aims of TEAC

1. Empowerment and Restoration of Spiritual Identity

To empower individuals to recognize and embrace their inherent spiritual divinity, fostering a community where members feel confident in their personal spiritual journeys.

2. Connection to the Self

To facilitate profound connections between individuals and their celestial guardians, ensuring that each member feels supported and guided on their spiritual path.

3. A Hub of Spiritual Knowledge

To establish TEAC as a leading center for learning and understanding African spirituality, where teachings are shared and preserved for future generations.
